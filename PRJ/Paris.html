Summer Vacation in Paris


Overview

Paris, the capital city of France, is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. The city is known for its cafe culture and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum.

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Sacre-Coeur and Montmartre
  • Champs-Elysees and Arc de Triomphe

Activities

Paris offers a variety of activities for tourists, including sightseeing tours, Seine River cruises, gourmet food tours, and visits to world-class museums and galleries. The city's parks and gardens provide perfect spots for picnics and leisurely strolls.
